Welcome to the OnoAir Help Page

Home page: In this page you can view the last minute flights and the future flights, where you can filter according to your desired dates/origin/destination.

Admin:

Manage flights: In this page you can view the flights and go to a specific Flight Page.

Manage destinations: In this page you can view the destinations and go to a specific Destination page.

User:

Book a flight: In this page you can view the future flights and filter the flights according to desired dates/origin/destination, and book the desired flight - go to the Book flight page.

My reservations: In this page you can view your future and past bookings and go to the Reservation details page.
